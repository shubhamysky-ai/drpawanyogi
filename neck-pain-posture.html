FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can poor posture really cause severe neck pain?

Yes. Forward head posture shifts your center of gravity forward, doubling or tripling the stress placed on your lower cervical spine. Over time, this leads to chronic muscular fatigue, spasm, and disc deterioration.

How long does it take to correct a forward head posture?

While pain relief can happen in 4 to 6 sessions, physical structural changes to posture take consistent training and lifestyle modification over 4 to 8 weeks. Compliance with home exercises is key.

What is Cervical Spondylosis and can physiotherapy cure it?

Cervical spondylosis is age-related wear and tear of the neck bones and discs. While we cannot reverse physical degeneration, physiotherapy effectively manages symptoms by strengthening support muscles and reducing joint inflammation.
