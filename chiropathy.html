Overview

Advanced Joint & Spine Adjustments

Chiropractic (incorporating chiropractic adjustments) is a manual diagnostic and treatment system targeting joints, muscles, and the spine. It focuses on the relationship between structure (primarily the spine) and function (coordinated by the nervous system), aiming to restore normal alignment and ease nerve irritation.

At Shri Shanti Physiotherapy Center, Dr. Pawan Yogi provides professional Chiropractic treatments. Through calculated, quick, and gentle adjustments to the spine and joints, we correct vertebral subluxations (misalignments), decompress spinal discs, relieve nerve compression, and restore natural posture, helping you feel instantly lighter and pain-free.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chiropractic and how is it different from physiotherapy?

Physiotherapy focuses on muscles, soft tissues, and general movement retraining. Chiropractic focuses specifically on spinal mechanics, vertebral alignment, and joint adjustments to restore nerve communication paths.

Is joint cracking safe?

Yes, joint adjustments are highly safe when performed by trained healthcare specialists like Dr. Pawan Yogi. The 'cracking' or 'popping' sound is simply a release of nitrogen gas bubbles from within the joint fluid during rapid decompression.

How quickly will I feel better after a spine adjustment?

Many patients feel immediate relief, reduced muscle tightness, and an improved range of motion directly after their first adjustment. Symmetrical, stable posture correction may require 4 to 6 sessions.
